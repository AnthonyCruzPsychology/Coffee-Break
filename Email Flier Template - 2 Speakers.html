Featured Speaker:

Dr. Daniel Ansari

Dr. Daniel Ansari is a Professor and Canada Research Chair in Developmental Cognitive Neuroscience and Learning in the Department of Psychology, where he leads the Numerical Cognition Lab, where his team studies the developmental trajectory underlying typical and atypical development of numerical and mathematical skills.

Featured Speaker:

Dr. Jörn Diedrichsen

Dr. Jörn Diedrichsen is a Professor and Western Research Chair for Motor Control and Computational Neuroscience in the Department for Computer Science and the Department for Statistical and Actuarial Sciences. He leads the Diedrichsen Lab, where his team studies human motor control and the human cerebellum.

Abstract:

Introduction to Bayesian Statistics

In this talk, we will provide a brief introduction to Bayesian statistics. In the first half, we will discuss the goals and theoretical underpinnings to Bayesian stats. In the second half, we will demonstrate how to apply Bayesian stats to t-tests and correlations using a prepared dataset.
