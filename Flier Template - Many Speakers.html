Western's Research and Scholarly Communication

Marisa Tippett, Kristi Thompson, and
Arielle VanderSchans

We don’t just sign out books!

Understanding how Western Libraries can assist you with your research and scholarly communication.

Ever feel like your research is getting bogged down because you have to deal with repositories and open access requirements? Wish you could stop worrying about things like finding statistics, data management, and publishing but still get all the benefits? Do you want to sleep easy at night knowing you’ve found all the relevant articles for your systematic or scoping reviews?

Western Libraries is here to help!

We have trained specialists who can help relieve your research stressors. Gain an in-depth understanding of the areas and ways the Research & Scholarly Communication (RSC) Team can help so you can focus on what you do best: your research!
